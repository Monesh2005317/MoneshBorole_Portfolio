Projects

Smart Zebra Crossing

A fully automated, intelligent pedestrian crossing system designed to enhance road safety and efficiency in urban areas.

Problem & Solution:

High-traffic areas often pose a significant risk to pedestrians due to poor visibility and human error. This project addresses the issues of traffic rule non-adherence, poor visibility, and ineffective zebra crossing design, which lead to accidents, by using a combination of hardware and software to create a comprehensive solution for managing urban traffic and improving safety.

Technologies Used:

  • Microcontroller: Arduino Uno
  • Actuators: Servo Motors, LEDs, Buzzer, DC Motor
  • Programming: Arduino IDE
  • Display: LCD screen for countdown timer

My Role & Contribution:

I helped design and build the prototype, connecting the Arduino Uno, servo motors, LEDs, buzzer, and LCD timer into a working model. I wrote and optimized the control code for barrier movement, signal timing, and alerts, ensuring smooth synchronization between all components. I also supported hardware assembly, testing, troubleshooting, and preparing project documentation and presentations

Smart Weighing Scale

A smart weighing scale using a 300 kg load cell, HX711, and NodeMCU to display weight on OLED and upload data to Blynk/ThingSpeak.

Problem & Solution:

Traditional weighing systems lack remote monitoring and data analytics. This project integrates a load cell with Wi-Fi/Bluetooth modules and cloud services, enabling precise weight measurement, live tracking, and database storage.

Technologies Used:

  • Microcontroller: NodeMCU ESP8266/Bluetooth
  • Sensors: 300 kg load cell, HX711 amplifier
  • Display: 16x2 I2C LCD
  • Programming: Arduino IDE, C/C++ (HX711, OLED, Blynk, ThingSpeak libraries).

My Role & Contribution:

I contributed to designing and assembling the hardware, including integrating the 300 kg load cell, HX711, and NodeMCU ESP8266. I developed and calibrated the firmware to acquire, display, and transmit weight data to Blynk and ThingSpeak. I also handled testing, debugging, and documenting the system to ensure accurate measurements, reliable connectivity, and smooth data visualization.
